Risk Assessment

Understand the risk levels in your legal documents

High Risk

Clauses with significant legal or financial implications. Professional legal review strongly recommended.

  • Penalty clauses
  • Indemnification terms
  • Liability limitations

Medium Risk

Clauses with moderate legal implications. Legal review recommended for better understanding.

  • Auto-renewal terms
  • Termination clauses
  • Governing law

Low Risk

Standard clauses with minimal legal implications. Standard review procedures should be sufficient.

  • Definitions
  • Scope and purpose
  • Recitals
